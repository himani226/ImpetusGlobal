About Us

  1. Home
  2. About

Impetus Global delivers high-impact solutions for both retailers and importers in their buying, sourcing and customer service needs. Working in partnership with our vendors, we service our customers in sourcing yarns & apparel ethically with competitive pricing, consistent quality, on-time delivery & provide them with our consultancy.

We believe it's all in the sourcing – those that matter most to customers and the careful attention we pay to all of the small details so our customers don't have to. One size doesn't fit all – and that customized solutions are the rule, not the exception. What truly makes us different is how we embrace that challenge to serve the diverse needs of our customers.

We've never aspired to be all things to all people. Rather, our approach has always been to be the preferred partner to our customers.

Our capabilities reveal technical expertise in yarns, woven, knits and sweaters, and our innovation and technical execution services demonstrate strength throughout every aspect of product development.

Our service embraces the production of merchandise in remote locations throughout the world and moving this merchandise directly to a selling floor or distribution center. We provide full and up to date information on markets throughout the world; identify and qualify suppliers and factories; provide comprehensive product development assistance; coordinate raw material supply; provide comprehensive on-site quality control; supervise logistics and multi-market merchandise programs. In sum, we seamlessly manage the entire global supply chain.
